<p><p>The spring probe market finds applications mainly in In-circuit Test (ICT) and Functional Test (FCT) for electrical testing of printed circuit boards (PCBs). In-circuit testing checks the performance and functionality of individual components on a PCB, ensuring their proper operation before assembly. Functional testing, on the other hand, verifies the overall functionality of the fully assembled PCB. Both ICT and FCT require the use of spring probes to establish electrical contact with the circuitry on the board, enabling accurate testing and quality assurance.</p></p>
