<p><p>Gratings for space-flight market types can be classified into two main categories: transmission and reflection. Transmission gratings are thin optical components that diffract light, allowing specific wavelengths to pass through while blocking others. They are commonly used in spectroscopy applications to disperse light and analyze its components. On the other hand, reflection gratings reflect incident light at different angles based on its wavelength. These gratings find applications in wavelength selection, laser pulse compression, and other optical systems where precise control over reflected light is required.</p></p>
