<p><p>Analog signal conditioners are used to convert and improve analog signals in various applications. The market for analog signal conditioners can be categorized into three types: single channel, dual channel, and multi-channel. </p><p>Single channel signal conditioners are designed to process one analog signal input, providing amplification, filtering, and isolation functions. Dual channel signal conditioners, on the other hand, can process two separate analog signals simultaneously, offering increased flexibility and functionality. Lastly, multi-channel signal conditioners are capable of processing multiple analog signals simultaneously, making them suitable for complex systems that require the conditioning of numerous signals.</p></p>
