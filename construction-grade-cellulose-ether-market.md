<p><p>Construction grade cellulose ether market is divided into three types: Carboxymethyl Cellulose (CMC), Methyl Hydroxyethyl Cellulose (MC/HPMC), and others. CMC is a water-soluble compound widely used in cement, mortar, and gypsum-based products, providing good water retention and workability. MC/HPMC, on the other hand, acts as a thickening and binding agent, enhancing the adhesive and water retention properties of construction materials. The third category comprises various types of cellulose ethers that have specific functionalities, such as improving crack resistance, increasing strength, or reducing air entrainment.</p></p>
